ABOUT

AMC Entertainment Holdings, Inc. is an American movie theater chain headquartered in Leawood, Kansas, and the largest movie theater chain in the world. Founded in 1920, AMC has the largest share of the U.S. theater market ahead of Regal and Cinemark Theatres.

PROBLEM

The Covid-19 pandemic had a devastating impact on the Film Industry.

The masses find visiting the movie theatre a hefty load on their wallet.

OTT platforms took this opportunity and started releasing engrossing exclusive content on their respective platforms.

Many of the customers tend to enjoy the privacy of watching entertaining content at the comfort of their home rather than in a public place.
